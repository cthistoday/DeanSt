Getting around

With five tube stations within 13 minutes on foot, Townsend House is one of the best-connected addresses in central London. The Elizabeth line at Tottenham Court Road gives direct access to Heathrow in under 40 minutes.

Numerous bus routes run along Oxford Street and Shaftesbury Avenue, and the flat is well-positioned for cycling — with Santander Cycle docks on Dean Street and nearby streets.

Street parking is available in the surrounding streets. The property is in a Controlled Parking Zone — a resident's permit can be applied for through Westminster City Council.

Council

The property falls within the London Borough of Westminster. Council Tax Band F. Westminster is one of London's best-resourced boroughs with excellent public services and street cleaning.
